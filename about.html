X-12 Series Project

The last X-Series project by xc11

Hey everyone! I'm Yaison (xc11)!

A student who loves exploring the internet.

This project is part of a series that I've been working on for five years now.

From the closed-source XC-X and X-11 projects, this leads us to the latest initiative, the open-source X-12 Series.

I understand there might be confusion about why there are no XC-X 10 or X-11 Series.

This is because I chose to close-source those earlier projects entirely.

However, with the X-12 Series, we're now inviting anyone to contribute ideas and suggestions, making it an open-source initiative.

I hope you find something here helpful for your project.

Current Collaboration

X-12 Series is now collaborating with LKKC Backstage Team

The X-12 Series will primarily manage multimedia control in major events, such as:

  • Singing contests
  • Interclass performance art competitions
  • Camera control
  • Replay management
  • Background music setup
  • RGB lighting control
